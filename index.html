🌡️🐈 Sick Kitty Care: What’s Involved?

Caring for our sick or recovering kitties is one of the most rewarding roles at the rescue! Here is a glimpse of what our Medical Care volunteers do:

🧼
Sanitizing & Maintenance

Scooping litter boxes, wiping down cage surfaces with rescue-grade disinfectant, and ensuring fresh water is always available.

🧸
Environment & Comfort

Swapping out soiled bedding for clean, cozy blankets and providing quiet, gentle socialization to help lower their stress levels.

📝
Monitoring Health

Observing appetite and behavior, and logging notes in the ASM system so our Medical Director can track their recovery progress.

"Healing starts with a clean cage and a kind heart!"

Sick Kitty Care & Maintenance

🧼 Cage & Litter Sanitizing

Scoop or change litter boxes and wipe down cage surfaces.
Safety First: Please use the Rescue Disinfectant and wear gloves to prevent cross-contamination!

🧸 Soft Bedding & Pets

Swap out soiled blankets for fresh, cozy ones. If the kitty is up for it, some gentle head scratches and "quiet time" go a long way for recovery!

📝 Appetite & Health Notes

Note any sneezing, lethargy, or changes in eating habits. Keeping accurate logs helps the medical team track their progress!
