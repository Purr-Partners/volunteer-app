Sick Kitty Care & Cleaning

🧼 Sanitize & Refresh

Clean crates, refresh litter, and ensure fresh water.
Tip: Use the specialized cleaner for the isolation ward!

🧸 Comfort & Socialize

Spend time petting and talking to the kitties. Low-stress interaction helps their immune systems!

💊 Log Meds & Observations

Did they eat? Are they congested? Log all meds and health notes in the ASM system.

Cat Return Protocol

Is it within the 2-week return window?

1. Paperwork

No new paperwork needed (original contract covers this). Ensure you get the blue folder back and re-print a new contract (write in microchip # at top), return policy, and checklist.

2. Notify Team Lead

Notify your Team Lead immediately as they may be able to discuss with the adopter and resolve concerns.

3. Resolutions & Notifications

If cat must be returned, ask if they want a Refund

💰 Email Barbara for Refund

OR

Different Cat

🐈 Email Judy to Swap/Re-approve
3. Final Step Once the cat is physically back, remind the Team Lead to reactivate them in ASM.
Official Return Process
